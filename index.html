About Us

Our Story & Foundation

Discover the journey that led to CNDA's establishment and the principles that guide our mission to serve the community with dedication and transparency.

Our Journey

Our roots trace back to the former Higher 16 Kebele 08, currently known as Woreda 07, in the Congo Veterans Settlement, Afaf Settlement, Asmara Road, and the neighboring settlements in Addis Ababa City, where we were born, raised, and educated. As we matured, reminiscing about our old neighborhood became a topic of conversation during various social events. This led to the creation of a Telegram channel, which attracted a large number of co-developers from both within and outside the country. Through the channel, we were able to exchange information regarding the wellbeing of our elderly parents and neighbors who reside in our old neighborhood. As a result, we decided to initiate a monthly fundraising program to provide financial assistance to those in need. Witnessing this philanthropic gesture, many individuals appreciated and joined in on the effort, including our family members, friends, and relatives. Consequently, we reached a consensus to establish the Congo and Neighborhood Development Association (CNDA) and legally register it with the relevant government body. We firmly believe that this will enable us to expand our outreach and operate lawfully. By doing so, we hope to leave a positive impact on our current and future generations.

🎯

Vision

Our vision is to bring happiness to society, particularly the underprivileged, by providing them with financial, material, and health support.

🚀

Mission

Our mission is to fulfill our civic duty by being available to the people, promoting their capacity building, and supporting them in becoming healthy and productive citizens.

Our Programs

Aim of The Association

The Congo and Neighborhood Development Association (CNDA) aims to achieve comprehen,eggssive community development through targeted programs addressing the needs of our elderly, youth, and disadvantaged community members.

👥

Elderly & Infirm Assistance

Provide comprehen,eggssive assistance to the elderly and infirm within our community, including monthly financial support, healthcare access, and social services.

💼

Youth Employment Support

Assist unemployed youths and adults in finding or creating job opportunities through skills training, entrepreneurship support, and job placement services.

🏥

Healthcare Centers

Establish and provide services for various centers catering to the needs of the elderly and disabled, ensuring accessible healthcare for all community members.

🎉

Entertainment & Income Generation

Organize various entertainment and income-generating activities for the association, promoting community engagement and sustainable funding.

🏢

Facility Management

Assume responsibility for government facilities such as offices, medical centers, schools, and recreational facilities, managing them effectively for community benefit.

🩺

Medical Care Access

Facilitate access to medical care for the elderly and infirm, ensuring no one is left behind in receiving essential healthcare services.

Donation for 2017 E.C New Year

New Year holiday support distribution for community members in September 2017. The society provided special assistance for the New Year holiday, ensuring vulnerable families and elders could celebrate with joy. Support included food items, financial aid for households in need, and coverage of essential expenses.

For example:
– Holiday food distributions (flour, oil, hen and eggs) for 100 people
– Emergency financial support for families in crisis
– Additional contributions for medical treatments

The association encouraged members and supporters (local and abroad) to contribute generously during holidays like New Year, to bring hope and happiness to disadvantaged households. All contributions were audited and reported transparently.

Donation for 2017 E.C Christmas

Christmas support distribution for elders and vulnerable families on December 27, 2017 E.C. A large donation program was successfully held in and attended by community elders, church fathers, members of the association, and local government officials. At least 100 families benefited from the program with joy and thanksgiving.

Distributed items included: – 100 chickens for 100 households (1 each)
– 1 liter of oil per family
– 1,000 eggs (10 per family)
– Fasting lunch for guests and elders
– Bottled water for participants and elders

This program allowed our community to celebrate Christmas with peace, love, and joy.

Special thanks to our continuous supporters, especially “our brother Mulualem Mahtemena and our sister Fozia Mohammed,” who made this celebration possible. We also thank our members and executive committee for opening their homes, contributing food materials, and volunteering their efforts to ensure the program’s success.

በ2017 ዓ.ም. ታህሳስ 27 ቀን በአደግንበት መንደር ታላቅ የስጦታ መርሃ ግብር ተካሄዷል። በዚህ መርሃ ግብር ታላላቅ የሠፈር ሽማግሌዎች፣ የቤተክርስትያን አባቶች፣ የማህበሩ አባላትና የወረዳና የክፍለ ከተማ ባለስልጣናት ተገኝተው ለአንድ መቶ ቤተሰቦች የበረከት ድጋፍ ተሰጥቷል።

የተሰጡት እቃዎች፦
1. ዶሮ ፣ እንቁላል 100 – ለአንድ መቶ ቤተሰቦች እያንዳንዱ አንድ ዶሮ ፣ እንቁላል
2. ዘይት 1 (አንድ) ሊትር – ለእያንዳንዱ ቤተሰብ
3. እንቁላል 1000 – ለእያንዳንዱ ቤተሰብ 10 እንቁላል
4. የፆም ምሳ ግብዣ – ለታዳሚዎችና ለአረጋውያን
5. የታሸገ ውሃ – ለታዳሚዎችና ለአረጋውያን

ይህ መርሃ ግብር ሕብረተሰባችን የልደት በዓልን በሰላም በፍቅር እንዲያሳልፍ አድርጎታል።

ሁሌም ከጎናችን የማይለዩት "ወንድማችን ሙሉዓለም ማህተመና እና እህታችን ፎዝያ መሃመድ" የበረከት ድጋፍ አድርገዋል። እንዲሁም አባላትና የአስፈፃሚ ኮሚቴ ቤቶቻቸውን በመክፈት፣ የምግብ መሸጫ እቃዎችን በነፃ በመስጠትና በመስተንግዶ ሥራዎች በመሳተፍ መርሃ ግብሩ እንዲሳካ ትልቅ አስተዋፅኦ አድርገዋል።

Door-to-door monthly financial support

Door-to-door monthly financial support provided to permanent beneficiaries. Our dedicated volunteers conduct monthly home visits to deliver financial assistance directly to beneficiaries who cannot travel to our office due to age, illness, or mobility challenges. This personalized approach ensures that no one is left behind and allows us to monitor the wellbeing of our community members. During these visits, volunteers also provide emotional support, check on health conditions, and identify any additional needs that may arise. The program has been particularly valuable during challenging times, maintaining the dignity and independence of our elderly community members while ensuring they receive consistent support.

ለቋሚ ተረጂዎች ቤት ለቤት ወርሃዊ የገንዘብ ድጋፍ ተሰጥቷል። የእኛ ቁርጠኛ በጎ ፈቃደኞች በዕድሜ፣ በሕመም ወይም በመንቀሳቀስ ችግር ምክንያት ወደ ቢሮአችን መጓዝ ለማይችሉ ተጠቃሚዎች የገንዘብ እርዳታ በቀጥታ ለማድረስ ወርሃዊ የቤት ጉብኝት ያደርጋሉ። ይህ ግላዊ አቀራረብ ማንም ሰው እንዳይቀር ያረጋግጣል እና የማህበረሰብ አባላታችንን ደህንነት እንድንከታተል ያስችለናል። በእነዚህ ጉብኝቶች ወቅት በጎ ፈቃደኞች ስሜታዊ ድጋፍ ይሰጣሉ፣ የጤና ሁኔታዎችን ይመረምራሉ እና ሊነሱ የሚችሉ ተጨማሪ ፍላጎቶችን ይለያሉ። ይህ ፕሮግራም በተለይ በአስቸጋሪ ጊዜያት ጠቃሚ ሆኗል፣ የአረጋውያን የማህበረሰብ አባላታችንን ክብር እና ነጻነት በመጠበቅ ቀጣይ ድጋፍ እንዲያገኙ ያረጋግጣል።
