About Us

Our Story & Mission

Our roots trace back to the former Higher 16 Kebele 08, currently known as Woreda 07, in the Congo Veterans Settlement, Afaf Settlement, Asmara Road, and the neighboring settlements in Addis Ababa City, where we were born, raised, and educated.

As we matured, reminiscing about our old neighborhood became a topic of conversation during various social events. This led to the creation of a Telegram channel, which attracted a large number of co-developers from both within and outside the country.
